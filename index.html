Process

HOW IT WORKS

01

Allow Camera Access

SignBridge asks for camera permission once. Your feed stays local — never uploaded anywhere.

02

Hand Tracking Activates

MediaPipe Hands detects and maps 21 key landmarks on each hand in real time.

03

AI Classifies Your Sign

Landmark positions are fed to a neural network that identifies the sign and outputs the corresponding letter or word.

04

Text Appears Instantly

Recognized signs are assembled into words and sentences on screen in real time — communication unlocked.
